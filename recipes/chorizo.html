Chorizo Recipe

Description

This is a simple and delicious chorizo recipe, that will feed 4-6 people. It is perfect for a Sunday dinner appetizer or to bring to a potluck!

Ingredients

  • 4 spicy choriza sausages
  • 1 table spoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves
  • 1 cup red wine

Steps

  1. Chop and fry the choriza sausages in an oiled frying pan.
  2. Add garlic and onions and brown with the suasages.
  3. Deglaze the pan with the red wine.
  4. Add your crushed tomatoes and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. top with parmesan, salt and pepper and your parsley and serve!
